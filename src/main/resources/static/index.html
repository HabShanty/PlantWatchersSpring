This site is designed with servlets and interaction is done through REST requests
This page is the only html on the website, (This is a CLI game) and it will instruct users on the basic commands
Bracketed items ie.. {user_id} are not bracketed but should be typed plainly into the url
Commands with an asterisks (*) are admin use commands

Endpoints & their applicable REST commands

Begin by creating a user account and then making a plant with a name, type of plant and your user ID.
Water and feed your plant to grow it to maturity, and then harvest it for points.
Become the best virtual plant grower in the world!

User Endpoint

This endpoint allows a user to create an account and view it's score

  •      POST /user
    user?create={new account name} will create a new account and id, remember your ID!
  •      GET /user/{user_id}
    user?user_id={user_id #} will show the users current score and username

Plant Endpoint

This endpoint allows a user to create a plant and view its attributes.
  •      POST /plant
    plant?name={new plant name}&type={plant type}&user_id={user_id} creates your plant!
  •      GET /plant
    plant?plant_id={plant_id #} Shows the attributes of the plant and a picture of its current growth stage
  •      *PUT /plant
    plant?{maturity, waterLevel, plantName or PlantType}={new String or int} will update the plant's attributes
  •      *DELETE /users/{userId}
    plant?{plant_id} will allow an admin to delete a plant and no user gains score

Actions Endpoint

This endpoint allows a user to take actions with their plant.
  •      PUT /actions
    actions?water={plant_id} will water your plant by 25 points.
    actions?feed={plant_id} will feed your plant and take some water, maturity gain is dependent on water level.
    actions?harvest={plant_id} will harvest your plant, the maturity level at harvest will return points for your user score.
    You can also chain these commands to water, feed and/or harvest in one REST input.
